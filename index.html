About Me

Currently, I am a Ph.D student in Department of Electrical Computer Engineering, University of British Columbia , supervised by Prof. Renjie Liao. My research interests lie in the area of Machine learning, including imitation learning, reinforcement learning, representative learning, and probabilistic graphical models, and their applications to computer vision and robotics.

Previously, I was a Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science, Northwestern University from September 2022 to June 2023, supervised by Prof. Han Liu. Unluckily, I had to quit my Ph.D program due to visa problem. I obtained my B.E. degree from Department of Electrical Engineering, Tsinghua University in July 2022.
